The First Page

My goal here started off as an independet online radio station. There are many challenges in the way of that my first being a limited knowledge of how to code such a User Experience. This is being made to promote my musical and artistic interest but many of the things posted here I will probably be coming across for the first time.

Downloads are currently not available and this is the only page with content right now. Over time you will be able to see a collection of all songs and artist who have been posted here as they grow in number.

No one here is selling anything on behalf of these artists and there are no ads tied to this website right now.

This website hosts constructive narratives, help highlight artists locally, and aggregate things that I enjoy.

If you see your video or content on this page or website and want it taken down please reach out to me here or on Twitter. This site does not collect ad revenue and nothing is being sold. this is a blog collecting the music of local artist and other artists of all medums that I come into contact with. Everyones contact information will be posted on each page and you should have seen a description above this paragrapgh including anything you might have missed. Thanks for helping to support new art!
